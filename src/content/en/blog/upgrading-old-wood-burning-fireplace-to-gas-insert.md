You've got a wood-burning fireplace that you stopped using three winters ago. The chimney needs a sweep. The screen is dusty. The opening just sits there. A Heat & Glo gas insert retrofits that exact opening with a controlled, push-button gas appliance, and most installations finish in two to four weeks from the consultation.

## What the install actually involves

Your authorized dealer measures the opening. The right insert model is sized to fit. The new vent pipe runs up through the existing chimney chase. Gas line is run to the firebox if it isn't already there. The insert seats into the opening. Trim work covers the seam between the insert frame and the existing masonry.

That's the install. No demolition of the existing chimney structure. No changes to the wall framing.

## What you get

Push-button operation. Cleaner glass. No ash management. Direct radiant warmth. Most importantly: a fireplace that actually gets used because using it doesn't require fifteen minutes of setup and an hour of cleanup.

Heat & Glo runs a Gas Insert Rebate program offering up to $650 off qualifying gas insert upgrades from a wood-burning original. The rebate is applied at the point of sale through your authorized dealer; no mail-in form, no waiting period.

Confirm rebate eligibility, current dealer participation, and your specific opening's compatibility with available insert models by scheduling a free in-home consultation through the dealer locator.
